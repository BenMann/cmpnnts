cmpnnts headlines

v0.1.0

Headlines reach from h1 through h5 and like all text elements are rendered with system fonts. They come with a predefined margin of 0.7em top and bottom. Headlines don't require any additional classes but can be extended with colors and other components easily.

No kidding — Lorenzo called off his trip to visit Mexico City just because they told him the conquistadores were extinct.

Jelly-like above the high wire, six quaking pachyderms kept the climax of the extravaganza in a dazzling state of flux.

Ebenezer unexpectedly bagged two tranquil aardvarks with his jiffy vacuum cleaner.

The July sun caused a fragment of black pine wax to ooze on the velvet quilt.
